About me

Hi, I'm Matt Kilness I have been creating art for as long as I can remember. With my art I tell the stories in my head, and once I put idea to paper the story becomes the viewer's to interpret. I like to use my family and friends in my art as inspiration and also reference. My hope is that as I put these stories out into the world people can see thier lives in my art and connect to the images.
